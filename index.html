What is Advancing UT Tech?

We let students produce the future of UT by working with the departments and organizations on campus build and deploy technologies to help UT Austin! Students join a team and work on a project of their interest.

Join Here

Who can join AUTT?

All UT students are welcome to join AUTT! This year, due to Covid-19, we are going to be online.

Join Here

How does joining AUTT help me?

Joining AUTT gives students experience designing and developing projects in an environment that mirrors the corporate world, giving them internship like experience.

Join Here

Why join AUTT?

Learn and experience the entire developmental process while making new connections.

Design

Work with community members and fellow Longhorns to design a tool to help the community.

Build and Tests

Build and test your design with your team. Meet weekly to share your progress updates and see what other groups are doing.

Deploy

Deploy your solution to the real world. See how it makes an impact in daily Longhorn activities.
